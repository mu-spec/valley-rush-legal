Privacy Policy — Valley Rush

Effective date: February 2026

Valley Rush (“the Game”, “we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the Game handles and how third-party advertising works inside the Game.

1. Information We Collect Directly

We ourselves do not collect personal information. The Game has no user accounts or sign-in and does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, photos, or precise location.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The Game saves your best score, coins, unlocked skin, and settings locally on your device so they persist between sessions. This information stays on your device. You can clear it at any time by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the Game.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Game displays advertisements through Google AdMob, including banner, interstitial (full-screen), and rewarded (opt-in) ads. To serve and measure ads, Google and its partners may collect and process certain data, such as your device’s advertising identifier, IP address, general (coarse) location, and app interaction/usage data. This may be used for ad personalisation where permitted.

Google’s use of advertising data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy and “How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services”. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device settings (e.g. Android: Settings → Google → Ads → “Opt out of Ads Personalisation” or “Delete advertising ID”).

4. Network Requests

When the Game loads, it downloads a 3D character model from a public content delivery network (CDN) to display the runner, and it connects to Google’s ad servers to request advertisements.

5. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Because the Game shows third-party ads, parents/guardians should supervise younger players.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Advertising data is processed by Google AdMob as described above for the purpose of serving ads.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated effective date.
